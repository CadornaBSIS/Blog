MLBB - BATTLETRIPS

By Moonton - September 21, 2024

Attending the MLBB - BATTLETRIPS event by Moonton on September 21, 2024, was an unforgettable experience that gave me a rare behind-the-scenes look into the world of professional eSports. I had the chance to meet top-tier Mobile Legends players and learn about their training and dedication. The event included a VIP pass that allowed me to watch MPL pro players compete up close, feeling the intense energy of the arena. One of the most impressive parts was the exclusive tour of the backstage area, where I saw the vast technical infrastructure that supports the tournament, from the massive screens and complex setups to the numerous computers handling the game data, Wi-Fi, and tournament logistics. It was amazing to witness the coordination and effort required to ensure everything runs smoothly, deepening my appreciation for the world of competitive gaming.
